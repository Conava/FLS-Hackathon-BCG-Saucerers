# 01 — Vision & Strategy

## The challenge (from the brief)

A European healthcare group — ~10M patients, €3B+ revenue, 9 countries — has strong patient trust and deep clinical data but is losing the patient interface to digital health platforms and retail players who monetize preventive health via diagnostics, subscriptions, and supplements.

CEO mandate: **launch a patient-facing longevity product within 12 months.**

Three structural barriers:
1. **Fragmented data** — no unified patient profile across clinical + lifestyle data
2. **Capability gap** — org optimized for clinical delivery, not consumer products
3. **Competitive pressure** — external players own engagement and data loops

## Our thesis

> **The only longevity platform where your doctor actually sees the data.**

Combine the engagement loop of Oura/Whoop (daily score, AI coach, streaks) with the diagnostic depth of Function Health (longitudinal biomarker trendlines) — wrapped in the clinical group's existing EHR, physician network, and EU reimbursement rails.

D2C players sell optimization to the worried well. We sell **optimization + safety net**: when something goes wrong, a real in-network doctor in your country calls you. No US startup can offer that in Hamburg, Madrid, or Warsaw.

## North-star metric

**Weekly Actioned Longevity Recommendations per patient (WALR)**

A patient who acts on ≥3 coach recommendations per week demonstrates behavior change, not just app logins. Year-1 target: **median 3/week across active users**.

Why not DAU or MAU? Those reward app-opening, not outcomes. WALR ties engagement to actual longevity intervention, which is what the clinic gets paid for (directly or indirectly).

## Monetization model

Three layers, designed to leverage the 10M-patient warm lead base with ~zero CAC.

| Layer | Model | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Freemium** | Vitality Score, basic coach, EHR view, 1 clinic booking/yr | Acquisition funnel — existing patients onboard themselves |
| **Premium — "Longevity+" (€15–25/mo)** | Quarterly blood panel, unlimited coach, tele-consults, supplement plan, future-self simulator | Core recurring revenue |
| **Transactional** | Diagnostic packages, private-label supplements (40–60% margin), full-body scans, genetic testing, specialist consults | High-margin upsell surfaced contextually by AI, not banners |

**Back-of-envelope:** 10M patients × 5% premium conversion × €20/mo × 12 = **€120M ARR** + transactional layer. No CAC. Defensible via clinical trust moat.

**B2B2C (v2):** employer wellness, insurer risk stratification. The 10M-patient cohort is uniquely valuable training data no startup can replicate.

## Competitive positioning — what we steal and what we uniquely own

### Patterns we copy from D2C leaders
| Pattern | Source | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| Single composite score | Oura Readiness, Whoop Recovery | #1 daily-return hook |
| Biomarker trendlines | Function Health, Thriva | Stickiest UI artifact |
| AI coach + journaling loop | Whoop Coach, Superpower | Passive data → prescriptive action |
| Bundled hardware + labs + app | Ultrahuman, Lifeforce | End-to-end beats point solutions |
| Subscription + auto-ship | Hims/Ro, Zoe | Recurring revenue + habit lock-in |
| At-home test kit onboarding | Thriva, Zoe | Removes clinic-visit friction |

### What only a clinical incumbent owns
- **Trust + EU regulatory legitimacy** — doctor-signed, GDPR-native
- **EHR depth** — decades of records, imaging, genetics no D2C can replicate
- **Clinical escalation path** — abnormal biomarker → same-day physician, all in-network
- **Reimbursement rails** — private + statutory coverage that D2Cs pay cash for
- **Physical footprint across 9 countries** — phlebotomy, imaging, scans already operational
- **10M-patient cohort** — AI training moat
- **~Zero CAC** on existing patients
